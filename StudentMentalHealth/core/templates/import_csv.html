How to export from Google Forms

  1. Open your Google Form → click Responses tab.
  2. Click the Google Sheets icon to open the linked spreadsheet.
  3. In the spreadsheet: File → Download → Comma Separated Values (.csv).
  4. Upload that file here.
ℹ️ The Timestamp column added by Google Forms is automatically ignored.
